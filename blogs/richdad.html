5 Learnings form the Famous book :
Rich Dad Poor Dad.

• Key points of Rich dad poor dad
• It’s completely based on what was helpful for me peronally and would also be beneficial for you as well.
• In this Book, Robert Kiyosaki, refers to his two dads namely Rich dad and poor dad.
• he refers his own father as a poor dad and rich dad to his friend’s dad.
• The Poor dad completed his education and had a Ph.D. and got many degrees . and worked as a professional Teacher. The Rich dad never finished his eighth standard.
• I will be sharing my learnings from Rich dad and his advices to Robert and Mike(robert’s friend)

1. Knowledge is Power.

• If you have a lot of money but do not have any financial knowledge like taxes , investing, etc , you would be in a great loss. even Cardi B , a famous singer regrets not taking any financial classes when she was young. and now , she has to pay huge money to other people to manage her money.
• He said, “It is not gambling if you know what you are doing. It is gambling if you are just throwing money into a deal or praying.”
• when you have knowledge for investing, you will do well. and knowledge doesn’t just come from reading some book like Rich dad Poor dad. knowledge comes with real life executions.

2. You will learn by making mistakes.

• you might loose money initially in stock market, but that doesn’t mean that you’ll always keep loosing . with every failure , you gain experience. Every successfull person you see today have failed in their life many times. But despite their failures, they didn't give up. They kept on trying and finally, after a lot of efforts, they became what they are today.
• Early Success is a Scam. Great things take time.
• Failure is a part of Success . People who avoid faikure also avoid Success.
• He mentions, “There is always risk in evrything, so learn to manage risk and learn from it instead of avoiding it.

3. If you hate risk and worry, start early.

- He says “If you start young, it’s easier to be rich.”
• It’s Okay to be fearful. One way to overcome that is to start early and allow the power of compound interest work for you!
• Even Ankur warikoo agrees with this statement. he said , “ if you start investing into stock market at the age 20, starting with just 500rs per month and increasing the amount by 10% every year then in 20 years you will have 13L. in 30 years , it will become 75L.and in 40 years, it will become 3.5Cr.
• it’s not the amount you start with but the consistancy you maintain through out your investing journey.

4. Do not Specialize yourself in a particular domain.

• Robert’s poor dad wanted him to become specialized like him in a particular domain even though that didn’t work out well in his own life. Robert believes the more specialized you become , the more you are trapped and dependent on that specialty. The world is evolving and you must be adaptive to the innovations happening around the world. and if you don’t you will be left behind.
• Robert says, Learning skiils outsiide what you think of as your profession will benefit you.

5. The way you think affects your actions.

• Rich dad believed that the words “I can’t afford it” shut down your brain. But “How can I afford it?” opens up possibilities , excitement, and dreams.
• This doesn’t just apply at financial level but also your behaviour. Extracting something good from a bad thing is something which will change the way you think about the world. being positive always calms your mind and opens up your mind to think of all the possibilities to get out of that bad situation.

So, these were a few of my learnings from The books ‘Rich Dad Poor Dad’. I would definately recommed you to give this book a read and I am sure you will learn a lot about Money.
Thank you for Reading!!
